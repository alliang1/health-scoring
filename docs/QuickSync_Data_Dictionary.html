QuickSync Data Dictionary

Nexus Cloud self-serve SMB product

This data dictionary documents the entities in QuickSync, Nexus Cloud's self-serve SMB product acquired last year. QuickSync stores all customer data in a single flat table. There is no account hierarchy, no separate contacts table, and no opportunity tracking.

companies

Self-serve SMB customer records. Each company has one contact on file and a subscription plan.

Owner: Product
40
Total Records
11
Attributes
Attributes
Attribute Type Required Description
idPK Text Yes Unique identifier (e.g., "qs-1")
company_name Text Yes Company name
contact_name Text Yes Primary contact full name
contact_email Text Yes Primary contact email address
plan Picklist Yes Subscription tier Values: starter professional business
mrr Currency Yes Monthly recurring revenue in USD. This is the monthly rate regardless of billing_cycle.
billing_cycle Picklist Yes Billing frequency Values: monthly annual
signup_date Date Yes Account creation date (YYYY-MM-DD)
renewal_date Date Yes Next renewal date (YYYY-MM-DD). May be in the past if the subscription has lapsed.
usage_gb Number Yes Current storage usage in GB
users Number Yes Number of active users
Relationships

No relationships. QuickSync uses a flat data structure with no foreign keys, no account hierarchy, no separate contacts table, and no opportunity tracking.

Notes

Flat structure: no account hierarchy, no separate contacts table, no opportunity tracking. Each company has exactly one contact on file. The mrr field stores the monthly rate regardless of billing_cycle. Annual billing is mrr * 12 per year. The renewal_date may be in the past if the subscription has lapsed.
